Research Interests

  • Numerical Analysis
  • Differential equations, Integral equations

I think that maths is omnipresent and am primarily interested in using mathematics to solve real problems. I think that maybe in general, a real life process can be written in the form of equations that describe the relations between factors that govern it. A lot of these equations turn out to be partial differential equations no matter how unintuitive it may seem. .Solving these equations is of great importance as they reveal information about the underlying process. There are many methods already established that solve them. A certain class of solving them is by the use of numerical techniques, where the majority of my interest lies.
