For the chord progs, lowercase alphabet means minor, and uppercase means major.

The song is in B minor for the most part. The verse has chord progression G-D-e-D with some variations.

The chorus(?) has chord progression b-A-e-D (and then G-f#-c#dim-D-b) with some variations as well. The solos go over the same two progressions.

I have first recorded the progressions on an acoustic guitar.

Then I have played the recording back on my amp, over which I play an electric guitar.

I think this is called double tracking.

The 1st demo is sort of going forward and the 2nd one in reverse (not chord wise) if it makes sense.

The longer one is just demo1 after demo2. Please excuse some bum notes. Please use earphones if possible.
